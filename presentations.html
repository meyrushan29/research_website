Presentation Guidelines

Format & Resources

Presentations are provided in PowerPoint format (.pptx) for easy editing and sharing. All slides include notes and speaker guidelines for reference.

Accessibility

Presentations feature clear visuals, readable fonts, and accessible color schemes. Video presentations include captions and transcripts.

Customization

Slides can be customized with institutional branding and logos. Contact the team for specific modifications or presentation support.
