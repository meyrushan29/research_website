About Us

Project Overview

Well360 is a production-ready, AI-powered health analytics platform developed by a dedicated team of AI, computer vision, and full-stack specialists. The system delivers three independent ML-driven modules — Hydration, Fitness, and Mental Health — over a shared FastAPI backend, SQLAlchemy database, and JWT authentication layer, with a Flutter cross-platform frontend.

This project represents the culmination of extensive research and development across XGBoost modelling, deep learning (MobileNetV2, ResNet, custom CNNs), computer vision (MediaPipe, MTCNN, FER), audio signal processing (Librosa), explainable AI (SHAP, Grad-CAM), and mobile/backend software engineering. The platform ships 7+ trained ML models, 30+ REST API endpoints, and 10 database models.
