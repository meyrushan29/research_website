Hydration Module

XGBoost · MobileNetV2 · SHAP · Grad-CAM

Two prediction pipelines: a form-based XGBoost model and a lip-image MobileNetV2 CNN — both with full explainability.

  • 17-field health form with 20+ engineered features
  • XGBoost regressor (water litres) + classifier (risk level)
  • MobileNetV2 CNN lip-image dehydration detection
  • SHAP values + Grad-CAM heatmaps for XAI
  • Real-time weather integration (OpenMeteo)

Fitness Module

MediaPipe · Pose Estimation · XGBoost

Video-based exercise recognition with real-time pose estimation, per-rep form scoring, and 30-session muscle radar tracking.

  • MediaPipe pose estimation (complexity=1, FRAME_SKIP=2)
  • Per-rep breakdown with confidence scoring
  • Joint angle tracking and heatmap visualisation
  • 30-session muscle-group radar charts
  • Personalized body-balance recommendations

Mental Health Module

Video emotion detection (FER/MTCNN) and Librosa audio analysis, with per-user chronic stress tracking and rotating recommendations.

Overview

Project Abstract

Well360 is a production-ready, AI-powered health analytics platform built on FastAPI (Python) and Flutter. It delivers three independent ML-driven health modules over a shared JWT-authenticated, SQLAlchemy-backed database layer:

  • Hydration Module: XGBoost regressor + classifier for form-based water-intake prediction, plus MobileNetV2 CNN lip-image dehydration detection with SHAP and Grad-CAM explainability
  • Fitness Module: Real-time video-based exercise recognition using MediaPipe pose estimation, per-rep form scoring, joint-angle heatmaps, and 30-session muscle-radar charts
  • Mental Health Module: FER/MTCNN video emotion detection (7 emotions, valence-arousal mapping) with Librosa MFCC audio emotion analysis and per-user chronic-stress tracking

The platform serves 30+ REST API endpoints, manages 10 database models, and ships 7+ trained ML models deployable via Docker. The Flutter frontend targets Android, iOS, and web with platform-aware LAN auto-discovery.

Capabilities

What Makes Well360 Different

💧

Smart Hydration Tracking

XGBoost predicts daily water intake from a 17-field health form; MobileNetV2 detects dehydration from a lip selfie — both with SHAP and Grad-CAM explanations.

🏋️

AI Fitness Coach

MediaPipe pose estimation analyses workout videos frame-by-frame, scores rep form, tracks joint angles, and renders 30-session muscle-group radar charts.

🧠

Mental Health Monitoring

FER + MTCNN detects 7 emotions from video; Librosa MFCC Random Forest classifies audio emotion; per-user chronic-stress detection flags negative patterns.

🔍

Explainable AI (XAI)

SHAP values explain hydration predictions; Grad-CAM heatmaps highlight critical lip regions; per-frame emotion breakdowns provide full transparency.

🔐

Secure Auth Layer

JWT + bcrypt authentication with 50-hour token expiry, Google OAuth2 sign-in, and Bearer-token protected routes shared across all three health modules.

📱

Cross-Platform Flutter App

Flutter 3.0+ targets Android, iOS, and web; auto-discovers backend IP on LAN; uses platform-aware URLs for emulator, web, and real-device builds.
