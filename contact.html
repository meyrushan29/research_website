Help

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access the project documentation?

All project documents are available in the Documents section of the website. You can download PDFs of proposals, reports, and technical documentation directly.

Is the source code available?

The source code can be accessed by authorized users. For access requests, please contact the team directly with your institution and research details.

Can I collaborate on this project?

Yes! We welcome collaboration opportunities. Please describe your proposed collaboration in the contact form and we'll be in touch to discuss possibilities.

How long does it take to receive a response?

We typically respond to inquiries within 2-3 business days. During academic breaks, response time may be longer.

What is the best time to reach the team?

The best time is during office hours (Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM local time). You can also send emails anytime and we'll respond during business hours.

Can I cite this project in my research?

Yes, please cite the project appropriately. For the recommended citation format, please refer to the Final Report or contact the team directly.
